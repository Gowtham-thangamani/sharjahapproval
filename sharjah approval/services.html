Understanding Approval Requirements

How different Sharjah authorities work together in the approval process

1

Municipality — The Starting Point

The Municipality oversees building permits and trade licensing. Many approvals require clearances from other authorities first — a building permit often needs SEWA load approval, Civil Defense preliminary approval, and sometimes RTA access approval.

2

Civil Defense — Fire Safety

Mandatory for all buildings. Requirements vary based on building type, occupancy classification, and height. High-rise buildings and industrial facilities have more stringent requirements.

3

SEWA — Utility Connections

Manages all electricity and water connections. Involves technical assessment of load requirements, property document verification, and coordination with your electrical contractor.

4

RTA — Transportation

Handles road crossing permits, access approvals, and parking requirements. Particularly important for properties along main roads or in high-traffic areas.

5

EPDA — Environmental

Required for projects impacting the environment. Includes environmental impact assessments, waste management plans, and ongoing compliance monitoring.

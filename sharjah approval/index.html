Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our approval services

What types of approvals does Sharjah Approval handle?

We handle all types of government approvals in Sharjah including Municipality permits, Civil Defense certificates, SEWA connections, RTA approvals, Planning and Survey services, Environmental permits, Drainage and Irrigation NOCs, Telecommunication approvals, and various other authority clearances required for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

How long does the approval process typically take?

The timeline varies depending on the type of approval and complexity of your project. Simple NOCs may be processed within three to five working days, while more complex approvals like building permits or Civil Defense certificates may take two to four weeks. We always provide realistic timeline estimates during your initial consultation and work diligently to expedite the process.

What documents do I need to provide for my approval application?

Required documents vary based on the approval type but typically include trade license copies, passport and Emirates ID copies, property documents such as title deeds or tenancy contracts, architectural drawings for construction-related approvals, and any existing NOCs or certificates. Our team will provide you with a comprehensive checklist specific to your requirements.

What is your success rate for approval applications?

We maintain an exceptional ninety-eight percent success rate across all our approval applications. This high success rate is achieved through thorough document preparation, compliance verification, and our deep understanding of authority requirements. In the rare cases where applications face challenges, we work proactively to address issues and resubmit with necessary modifications.

How much do your services cost?

Our service fees vary depending on the type and complexity of the approval required. We provide transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden charges. During your free initial consultation, we will provide you with a detailed quotation that includes all applicable fees, government charges, and our service costs so you know exactly what to expect.

Do I need to visit government offices myself?

In most cases, no. One of the key benefits of working with Sharjah Approval is that we handle all interactions with government authorities on your behalf. We prepare and submit applications, follow up on progress, collect approved documents, and keep you informed throughout the process. You can focus on your business while we take care of the approvals.

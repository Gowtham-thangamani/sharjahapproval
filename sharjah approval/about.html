What We Know

Our Expertise

Specialized knowledge across all Sharjah government authorities

Our team possesses comprehensive expertise across all major government departments and authorities in Sharjah. This deep knowledge, accumulated over fifteen years of dedicated service, enables us to provide accurate guidance and efficient processing for even the most complex approval requirements.

In the area of Municipality approvals, we handle building permits for residential, commercial, and industrial projects, trade license applications and renewals, signboard permits, construction and renovation NOCs, and all other municipal clearances. Our consultants understand the specific requirements for different building categories and can advise on the most efficient approval pathway for your project.

For Civil Defense services, we specialize in fire safety certificates, emergency evacuation plan preparation and approval, fire fighting system approvals, fire alarm certifications, and compliance documentation for all types of buildings. We work closely with fire safety engineers and contractors to ensure your premises meet all SCD requirements.

Our SEWA expertise covers new electricity and water connections, meter installations, load increase applications, temporary power supply arrangements, and connection transfers. We understand the technical requirements and documentation needed for different load capacities and connection types.

In planning and survey services, we assist with land surveys, Affection Plan preparation, plot boundary demarcation, and coordination with the Planning Department for development approvals. Our team includes experienced surveyors and planners who can handle complex demarcation issues.

We also maintain strong expertise in RTA Sharjah approvals including road crossing permits, access approvals, parking NOCs, and traffic impact assessments. Additionally, we handle Environmental Protection Authority permits, Drainage and Irrigation approvals, Telecommunication NOCs, and various other authority clearances required for comprehensive project development.

About Authority Approvals in Sharjah

Sharjah, one of the seven emirates of the United Arab Emirates, has a robust regulatory framework designed to ensure the safety, quality, and sustainability of all development activities within its boundaries. Understanding this framework and navigating the various approval requirements can be challenging for businesses and individuals who are not familiar with the processes involved. This is where professional approval consultancy services become invaluable.

Sharjah Municipality

Building permits, trade licenses & construction approvals

Civil Defense

Fire safety compliance for all structures

SEWA

Electricity & water utility connections

RTA Sharjah

Transportation-related permits

Planning Department

Land use & development planning

Environmental Authority

Environmental compliance regulations

For most projects, approvals are required from multiple authorities, and these approvals often need to be obtained in a specific sequence. A building project, for example, might require initial planning approval, followed by Municipality building permit, Civil Defense fire safety approval, SEWA utility connection approval, and finally a completion certificate. Missing a step or submitting incomplete documentation can result in significant delays and additional costs.

The benefits of using professional approval services extend beyond just time savings. Expert consultants can help you avoid costly mistakes that could result in application rejections, penalties for non-compliance, or project delays. They stay current with regulatory changes and can advise on new requirements that might affect your project. They also provide valuable documentation support, ensuring all your paperwork is properly organized and submitted in the correct format.

Whether you are starting a new business, constructing a building, renovating existing premises, or simply need a utility connection or permit renewal, having professional support can make the difference between a smooth approval process and months of frustrating delays. At Sharjah Approval, we are committed to providing this support to all our clients, helping them achieve their goals efficiently and professionally.

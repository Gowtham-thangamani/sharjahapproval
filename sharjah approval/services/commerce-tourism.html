About Commerce & Tourism Approvals

The Sharjah Commerce and Tourism Development Authority (SCTDA) oversees all hospitality, tourism, and food service operations in the emirate.

Our team provides comprehensive SCTDA approval services from initial application and facility inspections to final licensing.

Our Tourism Services

  • Hotel & Resort LicensingClassification, licensing, and compliance for hotels, resorts, and serviced residences.
  • Restaurant & Food Service PermitsFacility approvals, health compliance, and licensing for restaurants and cafes.
  • Tourism Facility ApprovalsPermits for tourism attractions, entertainment venues, and recreational facilities.
  • Travel Agency LicensingTravel and tourism agency licensing and tour operator permits.
  • Hospitality Compliance & RenewalsAnnual compliance audits, license renewals, and regulatory updates.

Why Tourism Licensing Matters

All hospitality and tourism businesses must maintain proper licensing. Operating without SCTDA approval can result in closure and fines.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the SCTDA oversee?

The Sharjah Commerce and Tourism Development Authority (SCTDA) oversees all hospitality, tourism and food service operations in the emirate, from hotels and restaurants to tourism facilities.

What licenses does SCTDA issue?

SCTDA issues hotel and resort licensing and classification, restaurant and food service permits, tourism facility approvals, and travel agency and tour operator licensing.

What happens if a hospitality business operates without SCTDA approval?

All hospitality and tourism businesses must maintain proper licensing. Operating without SCTDA approval can result in closure and fines.

About Planning & Survey Approvals

Sharjah Planning & Survey Department is responsible for land documentation, plot demarcation, and urban planning across the emirate. Planning & Survey approval is mandatory for all land development, construction, and property subdivision projects.

Our experienced team handles all aspects of Planning & Survey approvals, from initial land surveys and plot demarcation to final planning certificates, helping you navigate the process smoothly.

Our Planning & Survey Services Include:

  • Land Surveying & Boundary Demarcation
  • Affection Plan (Kharita) Processing
  • Plot Subdivision Approvals
  • Building Layout & Setback Verification
  • Topographic Survey Services
  • Construction Layout Surveys
  • Land Use Classification Approvals
  • Planning Permission Applications
  • Height & Density Compliance
  • Master Plan Coordination
  • GIS Mapping & Documentation
  • Property Boundary Disputes Resolution

Required Documents:

  • Title Deed (Mulkiya) Copy
  • Owner's Emirates ID & Passport
  • Site Location Map
  • Existing Survey Plan (if available)
  • Project Proposal / Development Plan
  • NOC from Municipality (if required)
  • Power of Attorney (for representatives)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Affection Plan in Sharjah?

An Affection Plan is an official planning document that defines a plot's boundaries, permitted use and development parameters. It is a key requirement for planning and building approvals in Sharjah.

What does the Planning & Survey service cover?

The service covers land surveying, plot demarcation, preparation of Affection Plans and securing planning department approvals. These establish accurate plot data needed before design and construction.

Why is land surveying needed before construction?

Accurate land surveying and plot demarcation confirm the exact boundaries, levels and dimensions of a plot. This ensures the design fits the site and complies with planning requirements before approvals are sought.
